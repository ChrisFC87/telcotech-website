FNO Marketplace & ISP Comparison Platform

A comprehensive marketplace that connects Fibre Network Operators with ISPs and empowers end-users to compare and choose the best internet packages.

Coverage Map

Network Coverage Visualisation

Display your fibre network coverage with interactive maps. End-users can check availability at their address, view all available ISPs on your network, and start the sign-up process directly from the coverage map.

ISP Aggregation

Aggregate ISP Offerings

Bring all ISP partners together in one marketplace. Aggregate their packages, pricing, and services to provide end-users with a comprehensive view of everything available on your fibre network, driving competition and customer choice.

Lead Management

Manage and Distribute Leads

Capture and manage leads from your marketplace platform. Automatically distribute leads to the appropriate ISP partners, track conversion rates, and optimise your lead pipeline for maximum network utilisation.

Unique Campaign Tracking Links

Track Marketing Campaigns

Generate unique tracking links for each marketing campaign, partner, or channel. Monitor campaign performance, track conversions, and attribute sign-ups to specific marketing efforts with detailed analytics.

Field Agent Sales App

Mobile Sales Tool

Equip field sales agents with a powerful mobile app. Check coverage, demonstrate available packages, sign up customers on the spot, and track agent performance all from a smartphone or tablet.

Accessible on All Devices

Responsive Platform

Our marketplace platform is fully responsive and works seamlessly across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices. Ensure your customers and partners have a consistent experience regardless of how they access the platform.
