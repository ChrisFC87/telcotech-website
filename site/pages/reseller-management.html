Reseller Management Platform

Manage, grow, and empower your reseller channel with a comprehensive platform designed for scale.

Coverage Map

Real-Time Coverage Checking

Provide your resellers with integrated coverage maps so they can instantly check service availability for their customers. Real-time data ensures your reseller network always has accurate information at their fingertips.

Coverage Tiles

Manage Coverage Areas

Define and manage coverage areas using visual coverage tiles. Assign specific territories to resellers, track coverage expansion, and ensure comprehensive market coverage across your service areas.

Reseller Tiers

Tiered Commission Structures

Create multi-level reseller tiers with customised commission structures. Reward top performers with higher commissions, set sales targets, and incentivise growth through a transparent and motivating tier system.

Documentation

Digital Documentation Management

Manage all reseller documentation digitally. From contracts and agreements to training materials and product guides, keep everything organised, accessible, and up-to-date in one central location.

E-Signature Solutions

Electronic Signature Capability

Speed up your onboarding process with integrated e-signature functionality. Resellers and their customers can sign agreements, contracts, and forms digitally, eliminating paper-based processes and reducing turnaround times.

Digital Bundle Onboarding

Streamlined Bundle Setup

Onboard new resellers quickly with our digital bundle setup process. Configure product bundles, pricing structures, and commission models in minutes, getting your new partners selling faster.

Unique Configuration

Custom Platform Configuration

Every reseller is different. Our platform allows you to configure unique settings, branding, product catalogues, and pricing for each reseller, ensuring they have exactly the tools and products they need to succeed in their market.

Video Configuration

Video-Based Setup Guides

Provide your resellers with video-based training and configuration guides. Our platform supports embedded video tutorials that walk resellers through setup, product features, and best practices for selling your services.

Dashboards

Real-Time Analytics Dashboards

Monitor your entire reseller network from a single dashboard. Track sales performance, commission payouts, customer satisfaction, and growth metrics in real-time with visual reports and exportable data.

White-Labelling

Brand Customisation

Offer your resellers a fully white-labelled experience. Customise the platform with their branding, domain, and colour scheme so their customers enjoy a seamless, branded journey from sign-up to self-service management.
