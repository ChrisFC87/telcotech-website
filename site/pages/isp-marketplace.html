Custom Front-End Interface

A complete digital sales platform for Internet Service Providers with built-in coverage maps, sign-up flows, and self-service management.

Sign-Up Software

Digital Customer Onboarding

Streamline your customer acquisition with our digital sign-up platform. Customers can browse available packages, check coverage at their address, select their preferred plan, and complete the entire sign-up process online with integrated KYC and document verification.

Coverage Map

Integrated Coverage Checking

Embed real-time coverage maps directly into your marketplace. Customers can enter their address and instantly see which fibre networks are available, what speeds are offered, and which packages they can sign up for at their specific location.

Self-Serve Managed Platform

Customer Self-Service Portal

Empower your customers with a comprehensive self-service portal. They can manage their account, view usage statistics, upgrade or downgrade packages, log support tickets, and update their billing information without needing to contact your support team.

Lead Management Practice

CRM and Lead Tracking

Track and nurture leads from first interaction to successful sign-up. Our lead management tools integrate with your CRM to provide a complete view of your sales pipeline, automated follow-ups, and conversion analytics.

Price Comparison

Compare ISP Packages

Allow customers to compare packages from multiple ISPs side by side. Display pricing, speeds, contract terms, and value-added services in an easy-to-understand format that helps customers make informed decisions.

Field Agent Platform

Mobile Agent Management

Equip your door-to-door sales agents with a mobile-first platform. Agents can check coverage on the spot, sign up customers in the field, track their commissions, and manage their leads all from their mobile device.

Subscription Models

Flexible Subscription Billing

Support multiple billing models including monthly subscriptions, annual contracts, prepaid packages, and custom billing cycles. Our flexible subscription engine adapts to your business model and scales with your growing customer base.

Payment Gateway

Integrated Payment Processing

Accept payments seamlessly with our integrated payment gateway. Support credit cards, debit orders, EFT, and mobile money payments. Automated payment reconciliation and failed payment recovery help maximise your revenue collection.

E-Billing & Auditing

Automated Billing and Invoicing

Automate your entire billing process from invoice generation to delivery. Our e-billing system supports customised invoice templates, automated reminders, and comprehensive audit trails for complete financial transparency.

Website Development

Custom Website Solutions

Get a fully customised, branded website with integrated coverage maps, sign-up flows, and self-service portals. Our website solutions are built to convert visitors into subscribers with optimised user journeys and mobile-first design.
