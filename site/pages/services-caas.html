Coverage Maps for Network Operators

Comprehensive coverage mapping and management tools designed specifically for Fibre Network Operators.

Commercial Feasibility

Understanding Commercial Viability

Assess the commercial viability of network expansion into new areas. Our tools analyse demand data, population density, competitive landscape, and projected ROI to help you make informed investment decisions about where to build next.

Network Builds

Planning and Managing Network Builds

Plan and track network build projects from start to finish. Manage construction timelines, monitor progress against targets, and keep stakeholders informed with real-time project dashboards and automated status updates.

Bulk Packages

Bulk Coverage Packages

Process bulk feasibility checks and coverage assessments efficiently. Upload large datasets of addresses and receive instant coverage results, making it easy to qualify entire neighbourhoods or business parks in a single operation.

Self Manage

Self-Service Management Tools

Take control of your coverage data with self-service management tools. Update coverage areas, modify network parameters, manage access permissions, and configure display settings without needing technical support.

Insights and Data

Analytics and Reporting

Gain deep insights into your coverage performance with comprehensive analytics. Track coverage queries, monitor conversion rates by area, identify high-demand locations, and export detailed reports for strategic planning.

User and API Management

API Access and User Control

Manage API keys, user permissions, and access levels for your coverage platform. Our RESTful API allows ISP partners to integrate coverage checks directly into their own systems with comprehensive documentation and support.

Accessible on All Devices

Multi-Device Support

Access your coverage management tools from any device. Our responsive platform works seamlessly on desktops, tablets, and smartphones, ensuring you can manage your network coverage on the go.
